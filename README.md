# DAiLY TO dO ApP

## Description
Simplicity Meets Efficiency: An easy-to-use, user-friendly Todo app designed to boost your productivity by helping you manage your tasks effectively.

## Features
- **Effortless Task Management:** Just type and submit! Add any task effortlessly and never miss a deadline.
- **Task Status at a Glance:** Use the handy checkboxes to mark tasks as completed. Visually track your progress with tasks marked with a strikethrough once completed.
- **Instant Task Removal:** Delete tasks with a simple click once you've completed them, decluttering your task list and keeping your focus sharp.
- **Persistent Storage:** Leveraging the power of browser's localStorage, tasks are saved locally ensuring your tasks persist even after a browser refresh.
- **Clean and Minimal Design:** The intuitive user interface ensures a seamless experience for tracking your tasks.
- **Cross-platform:** As a web-based application, access your tasks anytime, anywhere, from any device with a browser.

## Usage
This app is a perfect solution for anyone who wants to organize their day-to-day tasks and activities, enhancing overall productivity and time management.
